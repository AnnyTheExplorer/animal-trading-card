Pug

A baby Pug has a greater probability to be born by caesarean section than most other breeds. They are born deaf, blind, and largely immobile. Thus, they rely on their mom, or breeder for food, warmth, and care. At eight weeks old, the Pug pups are developed enough - with their sight, hearing and mobility intact, to be able to leave their mom and littermates behind. They can then be sold and moved into a new loving home.

Breed: Brachycephalic

Size: Small
